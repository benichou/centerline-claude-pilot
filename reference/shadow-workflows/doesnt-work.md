# Things I've Tried That Don't Work Well

- **Asking it to assess credit risk or make recommendations.** It tries but the outputs aren't reliable enough and I don't trust them. I always do my own credit thinking.
- **Long complex documents with lots of tables.** Formatting gets messy.
- **Anything where I don't have good notes.** Garbage in, garbage out.
- **Asking it to predict what clients will do.** Useless.
