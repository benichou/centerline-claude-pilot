# Section 5 — Prohibited Use Cases

The following uses are prohibited regardless of tool or approval status:

- Using AI to make final credit decisions or substitute for credit analyst judgment.
- Using AI to generate content for regulatory filings or examination materials.
- Using AI to analyze or draft communications related to a borrower in Special Assets or active litigation.
- Using personal-account AI services with any bank or client data (see Section 1).
- Using AI to circumvent or reduce required human review steps in the credit approval process.
- Representing AI-generated content as independently authored to internal or external parties.
