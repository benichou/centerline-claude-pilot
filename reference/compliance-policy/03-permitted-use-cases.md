# Section 3 — Permitted Use Cases

The following use cases are approved without additional review:

## 3.1 Document drafting assistance
- Drafting client correspondence (emails, letters, formal notices).
- Drafting internal memos and relationship review narratives.
- Summarizing documents for internal use.
- Reformatting data for presentations or reports.

## 3.2 Research and synthesis
- Summarizing publicly available information about borrowers, industries, or markets.
- Synthesizing multiple internal documents into a summary view.
- Identifying patterns in data the RM has manually assembled.

## 3.3 Preparation support
- Generating call preparation briefs from RM notes and prior communications.
- Drafting meeting agendas or follow-up action items.
- Creating structured summaries of client interactions.
