# Section 6 — Existing Unauthorized Usage

The bank conducted an AI tool usage audit in March–April 2025. Staff who have been using personal-account AI services (including personal ChatGPT accounts) with bank or client data are required to:

1. Disclose their usage to their manager and to the compliance team by **May 30, 2025**.
2. Immediately discontinue use of personal-account AI services with bank data.
3. Migrate approved workflows to Claude for Work.

Staff who self-disclose by the deadline will not face disciplinary action for prior usage. Continued use of personal-account AI services with bank data after May 30, 2025 will be treated as a policy violation.
