# Centerline Bank — Artificial Intelligence Use Policy

**Commercial Banking Division | Effective: February 1, 2025 | Version 1.2**

Issued by: Office of the Chief Compliance Officer. In coordination with: Information Security, Legal, Commercial Banking Leadership.

## Purpose

This policy establishes requirements for the use of artificial intelligence tools — including large language models, generative AI applications, and AI-assisted workflows — by Commercial Banking staff. It applies to all relationship managers, credit analysts, portfolio managers, and support staff in the division.

This policy does not prohibit AI use. It defines the boundaries within which AI use is permitted and the approval process for new use cases.
