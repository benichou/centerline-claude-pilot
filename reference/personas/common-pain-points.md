# What All Three Have In Common

All three RMs cited the same top three pain points when asked what wastes the most time:

1. **Writing memos and formal documentation** — especially annual reviews and relationship review memos. Estimated 3–5 hours per memo, 71 required per year for Marcus alone.
2. **Chasing covenant packages** — sending the same follow-up email repeatedly. No systematic tracking, no automation. Each RM has their own manual system.
3. **Preparing for client calls** — pulling notes from three different systems (nCino, Outlook, personal tracking) before a call. "By the time I've found everything I need, I've spent 20 minutes and I've got 5 minutes left before the call."

**None of them asked for an "early warning system." That's the CCO's framing.** The RMs want tools that make their existing work faster. The early warning insight is visible in the data; it is not visible to the RMs as a need.
