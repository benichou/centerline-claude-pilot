# The Adoption Spectrum

| | **Sarah** | **Tom** | **Marcus** |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Current AI use** | None | Active (non-compliant) | None |
| **Tech openness** | Conditional | High | Low |
| **Workflow flexibility** | Moderate | High | Very low |
| **Influence on peers** | High (teacher) | Moderate (early adopter) | High (skeptic anchor) |
